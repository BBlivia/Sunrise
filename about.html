Our Story

Our passion and purpose is bringing
trend-facing, modern hair to Metro Detroit

Our founders grew up in the Metro Detroit area, and were struck by the lack of modern salon offerings for the community upon their return - so they did something about it. Sunshine Salon opened its doors to fulfill an unmet desire for high quality, contemporary hair care in the region.

Trained and apprenticed in Los Angeles, Chicago, and New York City, our founders envisioned Sunshine Salon to be a marriage of urban style and the warm mid-west vibe.
